The Problem

Many resources are consumed faster than they can be replenished in the technology industry. A report from MIT has showed that training an AI model is equivalent of up to five times the lifetime emissions of a gas-powered car. With the increase of AI technologies and other innovations being created, the number is suspected to increase. In 2019, the world has dumped over 53 million tons of e-waste and only 17% of that was recycled. Businesses must rethink their "life-changing" products by managing how they create and handle e-waste in order to meet their net-zero targets.

The Purpose

Sustainability is important to maintain so humans and nature can exist in harmony to support the present and future generations. With technology evolving every day, we can create opportunities to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, facilitate new business models, and produce products that serve customers but also assist with sustainability. Before this can happen, it is important for people to be knowledgable about the subject to help make a difference and innovate things that make the process efficient.
